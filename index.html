What is the Appeal of Kdramas?

Over the past decade, the popularity of Korean dramas has surged. Unlike most of what we see on Netflix, these dramas have a set format averaging of around 16 episodes, with each episode being typically no longer than 70 minutes. They are seen as comforting, consisting of a non-meandering plot that allows the viewer to become emotionally invested in each character. Contrary to many Western shows, Kdramas put the emphasis on “feel” rather than “think.” Learn more about how the concentration on chemistry and storytelling is unrivaled.
